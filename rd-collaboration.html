R&D Collaboration

Actuate participates in funded research and innovation projects as a technical partner. We bring a deployment-ready edge platform, direct engineering capacity, and experience operating within the project structures that Horizon Europe, Celtic-Next, and TÜBİTAK require.

We are not a research institution. We do not write proposals on behalf of others. What we offer a consortium is a validated technical foundation and the capacity to take responsibility for a defined technical work package.

Technical leadership capacity

In SafeRoute-6G, Actuate holds task leadership within WP3 — responsible for edge computing, secure data processing, and multi-source sensor integration across the project's field pilots. In an active Horizon Europe agricultural project, Actuate co-leads the digital early warning systems work package. We take defined technical responsibility, not observer roles.

A platform that exists and has been used

The Edge Platform has been deployed in operational environments across logistics, mobility, agriculture, and manufacturing. In a consortium context, this means the technical foundation is already validated — project work is extension, domain-specific application, or higher-TRL deployment, not basic development from scratch.

Integration where others stop

Our strongest contribution is in projects where the challenge is connecting what cannot currently be connected: devices and data sources using different protocols, serving multiple stakeholders from a single physical deployment. This is the problem the platform was built to solve.

Adaptability across TRL levels

We participate in RIA projects — extending the platform into new domains or communication environments — and in IA projects, where the objective is deployment and operational validation at scale. Current active projects span TRL 4–5, TRL 6–7, and TRL 8–9.
