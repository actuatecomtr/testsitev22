About Actuate

Actuate was founded in 2016 and is based at Marmara University Technology Development Zone (Technopark) in Kadıköy, Istanbul. Being inside a university technopark is not incidental — it keeps us close to applied research while staying grounded in the deployment realities of industrial and field operations.

Six people, one coherent technical system.

We are a small engineering team with tightly coupled technical roles across embedded systems, hardware integration, AI and signal processing, communication protocols, software architecture, and systems deployment. The platform was built in-house across all of these layers.

Our work is focused on one objective: an edge computing and edge AI platform that operates across mixed-vendor environments without requiring infrastructure replacement or vendor lock-in. We have not diversified into IT services, cloud management, or general software development. The platform is what we build. Field deployment is how we validate it.

Since 2016, we have completed more than 20 projects. Clients and partners include large enterprises, SMEs, and universities across logistics, defence, manufacturing, agricultural machinery, and higher education.

Some of those engagements have grown into longer-term technical partnerships. In selected cases, where a domain opportunity is clear and a credible team exists to build the commercial layer, we have contributed the platform as a founding technology partner — with the venture developing its own commercial and operational infrastructure independently.

How we engage

Direct technical projects

A client has an existing infrastructure and a problem the current setup cannot solve. We assess what is deployed, define the integration scope, and deliver a working system. We do not propose replacing hardware that functions. We connect it.

Funded R&D projects

We participate as a technical partner in grant-funded projects — Horizon Europe, Celtic-Next, TÜBİTAK, and others. The platform advances through applied research and the consortium benefits from a validated technical foundation with demonstrated operational history.

Technology ventures

In selected cases, where a domain opportunity is clear and a credible team exists to build the commercial layer, we contribute the platform as a founding technology partner. We take a stake. The venture develops its own commercial, UX, and operational infrastructure independently.
